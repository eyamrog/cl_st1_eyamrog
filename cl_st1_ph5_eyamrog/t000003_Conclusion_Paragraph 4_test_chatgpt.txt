Fourth, the dual emphasis on mobility and the processes and practices associated with mooring allows for a nuanced counterbalance to narratives of detachment, dislocation, and precarious positioning prevalent in migration studies, offering vivid and insightful perspectives on the contextualized nature of moorings (and re-moorings) while highlighting the agency of the individuals involved. Furthermore, a meticulous examination of the linguistic, cultural, and other semiotic practices of specific groups, such as the football clubs discussed in this article, facilitates a deeper understanding of the intricate ways in which local forms of mooring activities acquire rich, multi-layered social meanings.
