The family is cosmopolitan in distribution, comprising eleven genera and approximately 270 species (PPG I 2016; Shang et al. 2018; Schwartsburd et al. 2020). The clade that includes Dennstaedtia, Leptolepia, Microlepia, and Oenotrichia requires further recircumscription due to the polyphyletic nature of Dennstaedtia (Schwartsburd et al. 2020).
